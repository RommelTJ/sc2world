    About SC2 GeoMap


    SC2 GeoMap is the simple way of finding StarCraft 2 players all over the world. We provide a map where players voluntarily disclose their geographical location.

    Why?
    Living in Mexico, it was very difficult for me to find SC2 players to have LAN parties with. I started a Mexican StarCraft 2 community website at sc2mx.com but I still had troubles finding SC2 players. I noticed that many players across the various community websites organized LAN parties but there was no organized directory of SC2 players worldwide. I decided to fix that. Furthermore, SC2 GeoMaps wants to provide a visual representation of SC2 Players so that businesses, advertisers, and others who otherwise support eSports can better target their markets.

    Who?
    This site was created by me, Rommel Rico, a Computer Science student at the University of San Diego. I can be reached at rico.r-10_AT_sandiego.edu. The site, however, belongs to the community. I am simply the admin. Also, this site was based on the work of Kegan from MalaysiaCrime.com, so don't give me too much credit.

    How?
    Users are responsible for adding or updating new player locations on the map. The administrator is in charge of maintenance and the like.

    Objectives:
    My objectives are too provide a fun and safe interface for SC2 players to meet other players and to help promote eSports all over the world by giving a visual representation to possible sponsors.
